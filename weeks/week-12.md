This is the final week of the course. As you compile your portfolio and write your reflection, take stock of what you've learned about teaching, about AI, and about yourself as an educator. The readings this week look forward — at the future of education in a world shaped by AI.

## Assignments

- **Final Portfolio & Reflection** (160 points) — Your final portfolio should compile: your course syllabus, your signature assignment with rubric, your AI exercise, and your teaching statement. The accompanying 750-1000 word reflection should address what you learned about teaching in the age of AI, how your thinking changed over the semester, and what you plan to do next. Due August 1.
